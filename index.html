Aarshvi Gajjar

I am second year Ph.D. student of computer science at the Algorithms and Foundations Group of NYU's Tandon School of Engineering. Here, I am advised by Christopher Musco and Chinmay Hegde.

Prior to NYU, I received my masters from UMass Amherst in 2021, where I worked on my Masters Thesis with Cameron Musco. Before that I received my bachelor's from IIIT Hyderabad in 2016.

I am interested in studying the mathematical foundations of machine learning, specifically in approximation theory, randomized sampling, high dimensional geometry with applications to scientific computing and compressed sensing.
